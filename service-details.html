Agricultural Data Analytics & MEL Consulting

Transform raw field data into actionable intelligence that improves farmer outcomes, optimizes operations, and scales impact across agricultural development programs.

Empowering Agricultural Decisions Through Data

With over 8 years of experience in Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL) at One Acre Fund, I specialize in building and managing data systems that directly support smallholder farmers across East Africa. My expertise bridges the gap between complex agricultural data and practical, actionable insights that drive program success.

From leading field data collection teams of 50+ staff to designing real-time Power BI dashboards, my work ensures that agricultural leaders make decisions based on accurate, timely, and actionable information. I help organizations establish quality control systems, reduce survey errors, and create comprehensive monitoring frameworks that improve farmer outcomes.
